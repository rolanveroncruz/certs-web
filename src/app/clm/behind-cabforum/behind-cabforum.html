Behind the CA/B Forum's vote for shorter certificate lifespans:
Strengthening Security
Shorter lifespans means a shorter window of risk; but adds the difficulty of maintaining more renewals.
Post-Quantum Readiness
Quantum computing is on the horizon, so the need for crypto-agility will only grow from now.
Expanding Uses Cases
Our processes need to scale to the increasing number of digital and machine identities, not just SSL/TLS.
